    What we do ?



    Register as a Songwriter Internationally

    We register you as a “songwriter” (Author/Composer) for music publishing royalties across the globe.

    Register unlimited…

    We’ll register your 100% catalog with clear copyright in your name for licensing and publishing royalties across the globe, while our absolute professional service always lets you create new creations and register them hassle free

    Keep your right ownership

    Once registering with us while keeping your full right ownership, we will protect your copyright, collect your due license fees and distribute you for the agreed period of contract


    Fair and Direct
    Payments

    You receive 70% from publishing royalties and music licensing worldwide including Sri Lanka. You will also receive 100% public performing royalties for radio rotations direct from our PRO. We take 30% commission only from what you earn, when you earn.

    Bring your hits back to live Again

    Add further creative value to your songs through our catalogue remake pledge. If we manage 100% of the song copyrights, we will remake your selected songs to bring back its glory and make it heard for years to come with dynamic changes in the industry.

    New royalties & sync opportunities

    Discover new licensing, royalties & exposure worldwide. Earn every time your songs are performed worldwide, music is streamed on platforms like Spotify, iTunes and YouTube and pitch your tracks for TV, film, Gaming and advertising placements.
